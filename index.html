mycelial net

The Mycelial Net is a decentralized network and learning commons rooted in the principles of rooted pedagogy, community action, and the democratization of education. Like fungal networks interwoven into the soil of the Earth, we work to facilitate mutual aid and underground activism by investing into the relational rootwork that mobilizes activism locally.

environmental/radical education hub, environmental activist network, activist organization mapping

A tool for increasing educational access and interconnectivity to encourage activism. Conceptually based on the “root” network continuously modeled and encouraged by Professor Lewis.
